Our Story

Founded in 1995, The Modern Frame has been at the forefront of contemporary art for nearly three decades. Our mission is to bridge the gap between emerging talent and established collectors, creating a space where artistic innovation thrives.

What started as a small showcase for local artists has evolved into one of the city's most respected contemporary art destinations. We pride ourselves on discovering and nurturing new talent while maintaining relationships with internationally recognized artists.

Featured Artists

Over the years, we've had the privilege of showcasing works by numerous acclaimed artists who have gone on to achieve international recognition. Our artist development program has launched careers and provided platforms for creative expression.

Some of our most celebrated artists include contemporary painters, digital artists, sculptors, and mixed-media creators who continue to push the boundaries of artistic expression. We maintain ongoing relationships with many artists, often featuring their evolving work across multiple exhibitions.

Notable artists who have exhibited at The Modern Frame include Elena Rodriguez, Marcus Chen, Sarah Williams, David Thompson, and Lisa Park, among many others.
